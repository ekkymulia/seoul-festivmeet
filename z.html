About Broccoli

Broccoli is a type of flower vegetable where the flower is consumed. Most broccoli sold commercially has the leaves removed, with only the flower buds being sold. When examining broccoli first, check if the buds are plump, small, and dark green. If they have turned yellow, they are old.
